## HC-05 Bluetooth Module
- VCC -> 5V on Arduino
- GND -> GND on Arduino
- TX -> RX (Pin 0 on Arduino)
- RX -> TX (Pin 1 on Arduino) [use voltage divider]

## L298N Motor Driver
- IN1 -> Pin 8
- IN2 -> Pin 7
- IN3 -> Pin 6
- IN4 -> Pin 5
- ENA -> Pin 9
- ENB -> Pin 10
- VCC -> Battery +
- GND -> Battery - and Arduino GND

## Motors
- Left Motor -> L298N Output A
- Right Motor -> L298N Output B
